Summary

Cyther.online is a tool designed for Dungeon Masters to help them manage their campaigns. It is designed to be used in conjunction with the Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition ruleset, however, future updates will allow for wider flexibility.

Individual users may create campaigns and invite other users to join them. Users who create a campaign become a Dungeon Master of that campaign, and can manage users, characters, lore, and unique rule sets. The DM can update character sheets, create and manage monsters, and create and manage items. Updates to the character sheets reflect live, and your users can see the changes you make in real time during your game nights.

Cyther Online is still in early development, many new features are being added all the time, with some really cool ones to come down the road. Cyther Online should make a fun, useful, and optional supplement to any tabletop RPG campaign.
